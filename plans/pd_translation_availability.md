# Public Domain English Translation Availability Research

**Date:** 2026-03-01
**Purpose:** Identify pre-1928 (US public domain) English translations available in digital form for classical Greek and Latin works where Perseus has original-language text but no English translation.

---

## Rating Key

- **Availability:** EXCELLENT / GOOD / LIMITED / NONE
- **Format:** Clean text (HTML/epub/structured) vs. Scanned PDF (needs OCR)
- **Coverage:** Complete / Partial (relative to Loeb scope)

---

## PRIORITY GAPS REQUIRING ATTENTION

### Critical Gaps (No PD translation exists):
1. **Sextus Empiricus** - No complete English translation before Bury (1933-1936). Only Book I of *Outlines* covered by Patrick (1899). Will enter PD 2029-2032. Currently the most significant gap.
2. **Dio Chrysostom** - No English translation before Cohoon (1932). Vol 1 enters PD 2028. LacusCurtius has the text online now under a specific copyright interpretation.

### Partial Gaps:
3. **Hippocrates** - Adams (1849) covers only ~30% of the full Hippocratic corpus. The core treatises are covered but dozens of minor treatises lack any PD translation. Jones Loeb vols 1-2 (1923) add more coverage and are PD.
4. **Martial** - Bohn translation omits obscene epigrams. Ker Loeb (1919-1920) is PD but only available as scans needing OCR.

### Borderline but Available:
5. **Statius** - Mozley (1928) entered PD in 2024. Already well-digitized on Theoi.com and Wikisource.
6. **Gellius** - Rolfe (1927) entered PD in 2023. Already on LacusCurtius.
